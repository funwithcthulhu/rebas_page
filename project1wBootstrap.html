Welcome to Reba's Page

Reba has been adapting to her new life very well! Last spring
she got into a fight with a veritable army of scorpions, leaving her paralyzed
from the upper waist down to the lower waist. She takes it in stride though!

Poor Reba curled up in sheer agony over the paltry 14 hours
of sleep she got last night. I'm typing this extra quietly so I don't wake the
angel up. Sleep up, lil' pup.

The vet said this might happen: the scorpion poison can
confuse the small brains of smaller mammals, leading them to see inanimate
objects as prey. Luckily the venom has worn down her teeth to basically nubs so
she can't do any damage to the computer (or herself!).

Well, a sad update today. It seems the poison has taken
the penultimate toll: Reba's bodily has withered away and now she's basically
a mobile skull. Suprisingly, this hasn't affected her attitude or appetite
and scoots along the carpet through sheer anxious energy
